Important guidance

keep insights relative, not absolute
avoid overclaiming physiology or recovery state
do not prescribe future workouts
use safe language like:
“has been elevated recently”
“has been less emphasized”
“training has leaned toward”
be conservative when data quality is weak or fallback usage is high
keep outputs deterministic and explainable
