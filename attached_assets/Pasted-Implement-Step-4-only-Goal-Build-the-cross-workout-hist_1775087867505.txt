Suggested responsibilities

historyAggregation.ts
Main entry point.

Important guidance

keep historical aggregation separate from workout scoring
keep cumulative and recency-weighted views separate
use relative language like “underrepresented relative to selected range”
do not create hard-coded universal training ideals
keep outputs explainable and deterministic
preserve safe behavior for empty histories and incomplete metadata

Recommended V1 recency model
Use a simple explainable recency weighting model such as:

0–2 days ago = 1.0
3–7 days ago = 0.8
8–14 days ago = 0.55
15–30 days ago = 0.3
31–90 days ago = 0.15
older = 0.08

You may refine the bucket edges if the codebase suggests a cleaner implementation, but keep the model simple and explainable.
